🕹️ Controls + Tips

Master the basics before you hit the arena!

📱 Mobile Controls:

  • 🅿️ Pass – Tap left
  • 🎯 Shoot – Tap right
  • 🕹️ Boost – Tap bottom middle
  • 🌀 Trick – Swipe in any direction

⌨️ PC Controls:

  • WASD – Move
  • Space – Boost
  • Q – Pass
  • E – Shoot
  • Shift – Trick
